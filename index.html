About Jamie

Jamie is a Montreal-based professional hip-hop dancer and choreographer focused on competitive teams. He has been a hip-hop dancer since childhood and builds routines that look premium on stage while staying practical for real studio rehearsal timelines.

As the founder of VISION Dance Camp, Jamie brings both creative direction and operational clarity, helping studio owners, directors, and dancers align around a stronger final result.
